{% block title %} A daily exercise to see things with new eyes. {% endblock %}



Dedicate 5 minutes each day to really notice the details of the world around you.

Each exercise you will be presented with a new random image and a timer for 5 minutes. During this period, write down all of the detail that you can see in the image. From foreground to background. From obvious to detail. From objective to emotional response.

The goal of the exercise is to refresh and renew your lens to the world.
